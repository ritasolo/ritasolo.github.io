writing

words words words words.

01.

questions

i. how to find balance
between allowing and
controlling.

ii. how to witness each moment
and let it be as it is
completely.

iii. how to make choices
with full integrity and truth
and then let them go.

02.

to remember in the fire

lean back into the support
of family and guides
both invisible and incarnated
whether blood or kindred
who are rooting for you.
your path and your learning.

03.

ode to maia

birth / rebirth
your transformation from
water to air
inner to outer
star to human.

aquarian
pouring wisdom
from heaven to earth
water bearer
water child

you are a blessing I receive
through the shift of centre
through the expansion of the heart.

04.

⦚ ⦚ ⦚

simple and clean
just here, as I am
with the clarity of vision
wide eyes and a steady gaze.
without burden, without need,
freedom in the gratitude of what is.

considering the possibility that
in every moment, I am where I need to be.
listening to the inner sense
the choices, the guidance,
the path of this experience.
how real, how fleeting.

the cycles – how many times before
have I passed through this place?
how many times
have mother and father,
earth and sky, kissed,
and granted me another round?
to learn, over and over
how to ride the waves,
how to walk with grace,
how to depart this plane,
with a head cleaner
and heart more sure
than when I arrived.
